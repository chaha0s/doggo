MAWF

MAWF, Maheshwari Animal Welfare Foundation, incorporated as a Section 8 company of Income Tax Act,1961 by Mr Ajay And Mrs Varsha Maheshwari of Nagpur. This Company plans to do its’ best in the field of Animal Welfare and for the Underprivileged of the society.

A Mega Mission With Clear Vision

The Mega Mission of MAWF can be understood precisely in the phrase that “The Animals Are Not A Subject Of Charity . The Animals Are A Subject Of Duty & Responsibility”.
Animals are not for exploitation at the cost of their liberty and natural rights of food & shelter. To be a noble society , we must treat animals as our natural cohabitants & and must look after all their needs and necessities. Following the aforesaid truths , the MAWF has following goals and objects :-
- A quick reacting , rapid action team ( well facilitated with the facilities of ambulances etc) to attend the call of any injured dog or cow.
- A fully facilitated modern hospital in each district ‘exclusively dedicated to the stray animals’ along with attached shelters for post treatment care, food & shelter needs.
- All stray animals to be taken care of for food , shelter, medical treatment, vaccination, sterilization , parenting and adoption and finally a happy & healthy settlement for all of them.
-‘All’ animals , living in human society, to get a safe, secure and healthy atmosphere for them. No torture, No Killings, No Exploitation. Can we imagine a world like this in India ? Yes We can. Come & Join MAWF. It is possible.

Ways to Achieve the Goals Eternally
Yes there are many sincere ways to achieve the “ultimate goals” as conceived by the MAWF :-
Corporates, millionaire & the billionaire

Corporates, millionaire & the billionaire are the people who can adopt cities for installation of animal hospitals, ambulances and animal shelters. And later the said institutes can be run by nominal contribution by each citizen. A city having a population of say 1 Lac can contribute Rs. 5-10 per person to successfully run the institutes.

City’s leading persons

City’s leading persons along with other interested people can make mutual contributions to install the required facilities in their respective cities and run them with nominal contribution for running expenses every month.

Muncipalities and the Government

Muncipalities and the Government can contribute to this project by providing Land and necessary infrastructures and making relevant laws in this most needy area.
Make contribution ( in money or in kind ) to the concerted efforts of MAWF and get the earliest outputs. 

People like you

Strive to make the Animal Welfare a mass movement. Let every person understand that the animals too have their right to survival, right to food and shelter & liberty at the same time.

Green India Clean India-


MAWF invites all the concerned citizens to join MAWF in its’ Ultimate Mega Mission of making India clean and fully green. Under this mission, MAWF invite volunteers to come on the streets and clean the dirty areas of the city, villages , schools , offices, government buildings and rivers and nullahs.
