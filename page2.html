Join MAWF 

MAWF is a most sincere effort to make grassroot change in the pathetic condition of animals and to make India a clean and green India. This mission can not be achieved on an individual or institutional level. This mission can be achieved only by a mass movement. And the selfless service of volunteers. The things will start to change soon with collected and concerted efforts by every citizen. Let us transform our country , the early the better.
Donation / contribution :- MAWF would be happy to receive donations from the concerned and willing citizens with specific targets to achieve by their donations. Corporates and business firms may opt for sponsorships of hospitals , shelters, ambulances , vaccination programmes, sterilization drives, adoptions etc etc in their names . This will give a good name and fame to the willing corporates on long long term basis. Similarly , financially sound individuals or groups of willing individuals can come forward to install and provide the necessary facilities with their names. Our ultimate mission is bring a positive change in the society towards the welfare of the animals. Willing persons can contribute to the efforts of MAWF by making possible donations. Donation Link  

Membership Oath

I most sincerely, from the depth of my heart, declare that
1. I am animal lover and I have firm belief , by depth of my heart , in life, liberty & rights of animals including the right to life, food , shelter and medical treatment & freedom from capture, torture or exploitation .
2. I undertake to observe the conditions of animals whenever I come across to see them and if I find any animal in trouble, I will call to the concerned agencies for help & rescue of that animal and follow till the animal is out of trouble.
3. I will always keep saved in my mobile the numbers of animal rescue agencies of my city, which are reliable for quick services . I would also keep regular numbers of some of Veterinary doctors of my city.
4. I will try my best to feed the stray dogs and cows of my street and other hungry & needy animals ( at best of my capacity)
5. I will fight for more effective laws against animal cruelty.
6. I will treat the animals with respect and dignity and educate others also to behave in similar manner.
7. I want to be an active member of MAWF. I would like to contribute in the Mega Mission of MAWF by :-
(i) I want to be an active and concerned member of MAWF. I am ready to pay the minimum membership fees of Rs. 100 only .
(ii) My active participation in all projects of MAWF [ Animal Welfare And (Or) Green India Clean India]
(iii) I would like to contribute financially on monthly basis as per my capacity . Rs. 500 / 1000 / 2000 / 5000 OR 5000 + Amount per month .
(iv) I would like to sponsor particular target works of my choice in consultation with MAWF team
(v) I would like to sponsor activities of MAWF as and when contacted to me .
(vi) I am willing to cooperate in any other way of my choice.
